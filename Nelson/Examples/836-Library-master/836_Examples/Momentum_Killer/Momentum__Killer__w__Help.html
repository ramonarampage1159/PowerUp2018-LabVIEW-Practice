Momentum Killer w Help

This VI operates as a motor brake to bring mechanical motion to a quicker stop than usual. When commanded by KILL CONTROL, the POWER OUTPUT is reversed for a short time to counter the momentum of the motor and it's rotating group. It is similar to using the passive shorting brake on a motor controller, except that this is a must faster active brake controlled by the setting of REVERSE POWER and REVERSE TIME.
