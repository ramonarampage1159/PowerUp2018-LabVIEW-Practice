hmi_cheesy_drive_w_Help.vi

Documentation by FRC Team 836 - the RoboBees

This VI generates modifed turn commands based upon speed, steering wheel input, gear shift selection, and if "Quick Turn" is turned on. When "Quick Turn" is on turn faster turn rates are possible. The "Drive Data" cluster is used by the "Drive_Loop_Cheesey_Drive_w_Help" VI to control the robot.
